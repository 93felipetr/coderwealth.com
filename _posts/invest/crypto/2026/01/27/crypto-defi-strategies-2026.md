## 1. Why Crypto in 2026?

### Maturing Asset Class

Crypto has evolved from a niche concept to a mainstream asset class. In 2026:

- Institutional Adoption: BlackRock, Fidelity, Tesla hold crypto
- Regulatory Clarity: US SEC approved BTC and ETH ETFs
- Tax Efficient: No capital gains tax in Portugal (if NHR)
- DeFi Maturity: Robust protocols for lending, borrowing, insurance
- Yield Opportunities: 5-20% APY available across chains

### Passive Income Potential

- Staking: 5-15% APY across validators
- Yield Farming: 10-30% APY (DeFi protocols)
- NFT Royalties: 2.5-10% on secondary sales
- Liquidity Mining: Earning fees for providing liquidity

### Risk Profile

- Moderate Risk (Like Tech Stocks):
- High volatility (20-50% drawdowns in bear markets)
- Regulatory risk (ban risks)
- Smart contract risk (exploits)
- Custodial risk (exchange hacks)

## 3. Bitcoin (Store of Value)

### Bitcoin as Digital Gold

**Why Bitcoin?**
- Store of Value: Scarcity (21M cap)
- Network Security: Proof of Work (PoW)
- Institutional Adoption: BlackRock BTC ETF
- Halving: Reduces inflation schedule (every 4 years)

### Bitcoin Investment Strategy

**1. Dollar-Cost Averaging:**
   - Invest $500 monthly for 12 months = $6,000/year
   - Minimizes market timing risk

**2. Long-Term Hold:** 5-10 year horizon
   - Bitcoin historically outperforms over long periods

**3. Secure Storage:**
   - Hardware wallet (Ledger, Trezor)
   - Not "not your keys, not your coins"
   - Multi-sig wallet for large holdings

**Expected Returns:**
- CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate): 15-25% (optimistic)
- Volatility: 60-80% (significant drawdowns)
- Sharpe Ratio: 1.2 (compensates for risk)
- Risk-Adjusted Return: 8-12%/year

## 10. Security Best Practices

### Wallet Security

**Hardware Wallets:** Ledger, Trezor, SafePal
**Software Wallets:** MetaMask, Phantom, Daedalus, Coinbase Wallet
**Seed Phrases:** Never share, use air-gapped devices

### Smart Contract Security

**Audits:** Only invest in audited protocols (Trail of Bits, Consensys, OpenZeppelin)
**Code Reviews:** Verify on GitHub if code is open-source
**Testnets:** Always test on testnets (Goerli, Sepolia) before mainnet

### DeFi Security

**Rug Pulls:** Avoid new, unvetted protocols
**Check:**
- Developer activity (GitHub)
- Smart contract audits
- Community sentiment
- Total Value Locked (TVL)

**Impermanent Loss (IL):** Understand risk of IL in AMM
- Use established protocols (Curve, Aave, Compound)
- Monitor IL regularly (avoid negative IL strategies)

### Best Practices for 2026

1. **Diversify:** 40% BTC, 20% ETH, 20% Stablecoins, 20% Altcoins
2. **Stake where possible:** PoS chains (ADA, SOL, DOT, AVAX) offer staking
3. **Use DeFi Cautiously:** Stick to established protocols (Curve, Aave, Compound)
4. **Monitor IL:** Track impermanent loss in AMM positions
5. **Secure Wallets:** Hardware wallets, seed phrases safe
6. **Audit Smart Contracts:** Invest only in audited code
7. **Tax Consideration:** Understand crypto tax in your jurisdiction (Portugal NHR for Brazilians)
